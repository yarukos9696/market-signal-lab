# Overview

Market Signal Lab presents the public research layer of a proprietary crypto-perpetuals research and paper/live trading system.

The repository publishes **real aggregate results** and engineering documentation, not fabricated trades. Raw data, raw trade logs, exchange credentials, private model artifacts, and exact edge implementation are withheld.

## Core claim

The project demonstrates the ability to take a market hypothesis from research to a monitored paper/live-style runtime:

1. define an alpha thesis;
2. build point-in-time features and labels;
3. evaluate through chronological walk-forward / out-of-sample windows;
4. replay candidates with costs, stops, targets, and slot limits;
5. choose robust baselines rather than max-PnL variants;
6. connect research outputs to runtime inference, risk, and monitoring.

## Public evidence

The public repo includes aggregate WFO/OOS statistics:

- 782 aggregate replay trades;
- $2,653.36 net PnL;
- 40.15% win rate;
- $277.85 max drawdown;
- PnL/DD 9.55;
- 14 traded windows;
- 3 negative traded windows.

These are historical aggregate results, not a future-performance claim.
